Package Description

The net.osm.discovery package provides support for the publication of business components and subsequent traceble referral of business services across a chain of business service intermidiaries and service consumers.

Components
{@link net.osm.discovery.PortalProvider} PortalProvider provides support for the registration and discovery of business components.

Development Notes

The current implementation has been prepared as a proof-of-concept. Planned revisions include the introduction of a PSS storage sub-system for artifacts, generation of manifests, and the introduction of specific description types supporting directy federation across UDDI, JNDI and COsNaming services. Planned enhancements include the creation of a series of demonstrations depicting service discovery by client C1 from a provider P1, service referral by C1 to another client C2, service invocation by C2 against a service provider P0, and presentation of the complete service referral chain.
