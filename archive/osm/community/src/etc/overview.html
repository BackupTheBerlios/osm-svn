The net.osm.community package provides support for the persistent management of business communities and the association of users under business roles within the context of a formal roles based community structure.

Components
{@link net.osm.generic.GenericProvider} GenericProvider that provides support for generic resource creation and management.
{@link net.osm.community.CommunityServantManager} CommunityServantManager provides support the creation and management of businenss communities.
