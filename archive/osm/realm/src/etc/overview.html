Package Description

The net.osm.realm package provides support for interception of a principal identity and access control decisions based on the target object, principal, and operation name.

Interceptors
{@link net.osm.realm.ClientInitializer} Handles the establishment of an invoking principal identity.
{@link net.osm.realm.ServerInitializer} Handles interception of incomming invocations and the validation of the right of access of the principal to invoke an ioperation of a given object.
{@link net.osm.realm.PeerInitializer} Combines the client and server initerceptors into a single interceptor for systems acting in the role of a full peer.
