The net.osm.collaboration package provides support for shared multi-participant business processes refered to as an Encounter together with a set of primary business processes facilitating collaborative agreement and engagement.

Components
{@link net.osm.collaboration.EncounterProvider} EncounterProvider is an implementation of a business process that exposes a formal collabortion meta-model detailing state transitions and membership composition.
